Project Overview

This project focuses on developing an automated malaria detection system that can classify blood cell images as either Parasitized (infected with malaria parasites) or Uninfected (healthy cells).

Using deep learning and computer vision techniques, our system analyzes microscopic blood smear images to assist healthcare professionals in early malaria diagnosis, potentially reducing diagnostic time and improving accuracy in resource-limited settings.

🎯 Purpose & Impact

Early and accurate malaria diagnosis is crucial for effective treatment. Our AI system aims to assist medical professionals by providing rapid, consistent analysis of blood cell images, supporting clinical decision-making and improving patient outcomes.
