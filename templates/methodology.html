Methodology

Systematic approach to building our malaria detection system using deep learning

1

Dataset Collection

Blood smear images collected from microscopic examination of blood samples, containing two distinct classes for binary classification.

🩹
Parasitized
Infected cells
🩸
Uninfected
Healthy cells
2

Image Preprocessing

Resizing: Images resized to 150×150 or 224×224 pixels depending on model requirements

Normalization: Pixel values normalized to [0,1] range for optimal training

Data Augmentation: Random rotation, horizontal flip, and zoom to increase dataset diversity

3

Model Architecture

🧠 Custom CNN

Conv2D layers with MaxPooling, BatchNormalization, and Dropout for regularization

⚡ MobileNet & VGG16

Transfer learning with frozen base layers and custom classification heads

4

Training Process

🎯
Loss Function: Categorical Crossentropy
⚙️
Optimizer: Adam with adaptive learning rate
🛑
Callbacks: EarlyStopping & ModelCheckpoint
5
